📚 Publications 

<h4>Miller, E., et al. (2024). "Deep Learning Approaches for Small-Scale Irrigation Mapping in Sub-Saharan Africa." <em>Remote Sensing of Environment</em>, 298, 113-125.</h4>
<p>Novel CNN architecture for identifying small-scale irrigation systems using multi-temporal satellite imagery. Achieved 94% accuracy in mapping irrigation patterns across three countries in Sub-Saharan Africa.</p>
<h4>Miller, E., et al. (2024). "Community-Centered Remote Sensing: Integrating Local Knowledge with Satellite Imagery for Environmental Monitoring." <em>Environmental Science & Policy</em>, 145, 78-89.</h4>
<p>Explores the importance of incorporating local knowledge and community perspectives into remote sensing research, with case studies from irrigation mapping and land rights monitoring projects.</p>
<h4>Miller, E., et al. (2023). "Satellite-Based Evidence for Indigenous Land Rights Claims: A Case Study from the Amazon." <em>Land Use Policy</em>, 132, 106-118.</h4>
<p>Demonstrates how satellite imagery can be used as legal evidence in indigenous land rights cases, with a focus on documenting illegal deforestation and land encroachment.</p>
